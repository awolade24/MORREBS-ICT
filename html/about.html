About Morrebs ICT

Innovative Technology. Reliable Solutions.

Our History

We are established and registered with the business name: Morrebs ICT Solutions and Services on the 11th of November, 2020. We have been consistent over the past 2 years in deploying our bespoke ICT services in helping individuals and organizations scale ICT challenges within the city of Jos, Plateau state and beyond.

On the 5th of May, 2022, we rebranded and registered with the Nigerian Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) as a private limited liability company to bridge the gap in the provision of competent and dependable ICT solutions.

Over the years, we have weathered challenges and celebrated milestones, always staying true to our core mission: empowering businesses and individuals through technology. Our journey is a testament to the power of resilience, creativity, and the unwavering belief that technology can change the world.

Introduction

Morrebs ICT is a tech hub and training academy dedicated to equipping individuals, schools, and organizations with digital skills that prepare them for the future.

We believe technology is not just for a few, it's for everyone. Through our academy, community programs, and digital services, we empower learners of all ages to thrive in today's tech driven world.

Our Mission

At Morrebs, we are committed to serving you with integrity, excellence, innovative approach and love while we position ourselves as your preferred ICT solutions provider; to emerge as the backbone of ICT solutions in Nigeria and the diaspora.

Our Vision

As your preferred ICT solutions provider, we aim to address present and future innovative needs of ICT with our cutting edge tech solutions and we want you to be a part of our story.
